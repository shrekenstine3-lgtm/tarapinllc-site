ABSTRACT

Systems Hydro Flight is a remote-response firefighting product concept that combines a forward hydro-flight suppression unit, tracked support base, tethered water delivery, and remote operator control to extend firefighting capability into dangerous areas while helping keep operators farther from immediate hazard zones.

Section 05

TARAPIN System

A remote firefighting system combining a tracked support base, managed hose reach, and a low-profile forward suppression unit designed to operate where firefighters cannot safely go.

Forward Unit

Ceramic outer shell, control layer, inner formed steel pressure tank, and metered jet system for motion and suppression.

Base Unit

Tracked support platform with main supply input, hose management, pump and pressure control, and remote-response support.

System Reach

Tethered architecture extends controlled suppression capability deeper into hazardous environments while keeping the operator farther back.

Section 03

ABSTRACT

High-level abstracts for the current Tarapin LLC product platform.

Systems Hydro Flight

Systems Hydro Flight is a remote-response firefighting concept that combines a forward hydro-flight suppression unit, tracked support base, tethered water delivery, and remote operator control to extend controlled firefighting reach into hazardous zones while increasing operator standoff from danger.

OptoPac

OptoPac is a robotic emergency-response backpack concept intended to function as a modular support platform for lighting, integrated equipment, suppression support, and field utility within hazardous and complex response environments.

RoboSuit

RoboSuit is a protective robotic suit concept developed to support emergency-response, hazardous-environment operation, and future specialized missions through layered protective design, systems integration, and expandable equipment architecture.
